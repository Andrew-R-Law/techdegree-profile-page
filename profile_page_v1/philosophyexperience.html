Andrew, the Philosopher

Education and Experience.

Education

I earned my PhD in Philosophy from the University of California, Riverside in 2020, where I wrote a dissertation at the intersection of free will, philosophy of time, philosophy of science, and philosophy of religion.

Before that, I earned my Bachelor of Arts (cum laude) from Western Washington University, where I majored in Philosophy and minored in Economics.

Experience

  • Pepperdine University: Formal Logic
  • University of Southern California: Introduction to Ethics (teaching assistant)
